What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, and banking details.

Types of Phishing Attacks

  • Email Phishing
  • SMS Phishing (Smishing)
  • Voice Phishing (Vishing)
  • Fake Websites
  • Social Media Phishing

How to Identify Phishing

  • Suspicious sender email address
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Spelling and grammar mistakes
  • Unknown links or attachments

Best Practices to Prevent Phishing

  • Do not click unknown links
  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Verify the sender
  • Enable two-factor authentication
